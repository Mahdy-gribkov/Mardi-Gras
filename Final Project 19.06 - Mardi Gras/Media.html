Photography In Mardi Gras?

Many photographers from all over the world make their way to Canal Street in New Orleans on Mardi Gras Fat Tuesday. Typically, they stand behind roped off areas and photograph the parades going by. The key to making great imagery at Mardi Gras is to become part of the parade itself.
